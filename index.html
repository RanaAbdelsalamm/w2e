South Korean indie rock band !

wave to earth (웨이브투어스) is a band under Wavy. They currently consists of 3 members: Daniel Kim, Soonjong Cha, and Dong Q. In January of 2020, Wave To Earth released their first EP [Wave 0.01]. the session members also include Jo Jung-geun (keyboard), Jeon-min (saxophone) and Hong Seung-gi, an artwork member.
